Sea Freight Forwarding – FCL /LCL

At JEM Logistics Solutions, our team’s experience and knowledge has seen us establish ourselves as a force to reckon with in the freight forwarding industry.

Our team of specialist can provide expert solutions for all your freight forwarding needs. Our understanding of the complexities of shipping goods domestically and internationally and enables us to expedite the process while keeping the costs manageable. Our suite of freight forwarding solutions can accommodate your every requirement regardless of whether you have a Less Than Container Load (LCL), a Full Container Load (FCL) or a complex project. Our team of logisticians works to develop the right solution for your freight.

We utilize the services of major shipping lines to arrange shipping to and from all major seaports worldwide. JEM works to ensure that your sea freight is delivered on time.

We can accommodate your needs regardless of the size of your consignment. If your freight is less than a container load (LCL) in size, JEM can consolidate the shipment making it cost-effective for you. If you have a full container load (FCL) of freight to be shipped to an international destination, JEM have flexible sea freight services, economical solutions to suit your needs.

Working tightly with our trusted global partners we serve you the best international sea freight solutions that are cost-effective. The staunch support of our international network connections allows us to provide first-class, reliable shipping services across continents worldwide
